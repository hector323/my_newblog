About me

Hector Salgado is a full stack web developer student at Kickstart Coding. Previously, Hector worked as a claims specialist/analyst for the Federal government. After several years of experience, he decided to pivot his career into the tech industry. He wants to focus his time and energy at a start-up company where he can utilize his programming skills. Over the last few months, he began his journey to learn programming skills in Python by completing several online courses. So far, he has shown great enthusiasm towards learning Data Science and Data Engineering. He graduated from California State University of Los Angeles with a Master's degree in Sociology where he studied qualitative and quantitative research methods using sociological approaches.
